The Manipal Conclave 2018

Venue: Library Auditorium

  • Abhishek Upmanyu

    Abhishek Upmanyu is one of the finest young comedians in the country, representing fresh new talent on the scene. Known for killing it on stage, he hurls one cracker of a joke after another, thanks to his Shatabdi-fast delivery and unparalleled observational powers. His quick tongue (he thinks faster than he can speak) enables him to finish hours of material in minutes and is what gave rise to his brand new show, "Thoda Saaf Bol".

  • Atul Gurtu

    Atul Gurtu started his research career in 1966. He has been involved in experimental particle physics research. His group at the Tata Institute was part of large experimental collaborations based at CERN, Geneva, Switzerland.

  • Ganesh Nayak

    A professor, cyclist, traveller and much more. Ganesh Nayak an alumnus and professor at MIT, has cycled across India and aims to capture the essence of health and living.

  • Girish Narayandass

    "Girish Narayandass is the Head Writer at AIB. He started his career as a Mechanical Engineer but later found his true calling in advertising. So he became a copywriter and worked on some of the biggest brands. But then he found his true true calling in comedy. So he joined AIB, where he intends to stay until he finds his true true true calling. Also, when he's not writing sketches he's replying to Emails and writing bios in third person at 2 am" - Girish Narayandass

  • Kavita Seth

    A celebrated Bollywood and Sufi singer Kavita Seth has lent her voice to Bollywood chart toppers like Iktara, Mora Piya, Tum Hi Ho Bandhu among others. Her personal work with music celebrates Sufi poets like Khusrow, Bulleh Shah, Rumi, Mirza Ghalib. She has recently started a podcast titled 'Main Kavita Hoon' on her YouTube channel to introduce music connoisseurs with legendary and upcoming poets along with her soulful compositions.
